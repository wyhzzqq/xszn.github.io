# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the "[Xingshu](/)" website (hereinafter referred to as "this Website") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information. This policy applies to all information collected through this Website. If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please [Contact Us](/about#contact-us).

## I. Information Collection

We may collect the following personal information:

- IP address
- Browser type
- Operating system
- Referring website
- Pages visited
- Time of visit and other usage data

## II. Tracking and Analytics

We use Google Analytics and other analytics tools to collect your IP address, device information, and website activity data to help us understand user behavior and improve our services.

## III. Advertising

This Website may display third-party advertisements. Third-party advertising networks may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect your information in order to display relevant advertisements. This Privacy Policy does not cover the practices of third-party advertisers.

## IV. No Sale of Products or Services

This Website does not sell any products or services, nor does it support user payments.

## V. No Use of Remarketing Services

We currently do not use any remarketing services for advertising purposes. ## VI. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) Rights

California residents are entitled to the following rights:

- The right to know the categories, sources, purposes, and recipients of the personal information we collect.
- The right to request the deletion of personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
- The right to direct us not to sell your personal information in the future (although we do not currently sell personal information).
- The right not to be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.

## VII. General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Rights

Users within the European Economic Area are entitled to:

- The right to access, correct, or delete their personal information.
- The rights to restrict processing, data portability, object to processing, and withdraw consent.
- The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

## VIII. California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA) Requirements

We commit to:

- Prominently posting our Privacy Policy on our website.
- Disclosing the categories of information we collect and the third parties with whom we share it.
- Providing users with a means to review and modify their personal information.
- Explaining how we respond to "Do Not Track" signals (please note that we may not currently respond to such signals).
- Notifying users of any material changes to this policy.

## IX. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed at children under the age of 13, nor do we knowingly collect personal information from them. If we discover that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

## X. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated periodically; any updates become effective immediately upon posting. Please review this policy regularly to stay informed of the latest content.
